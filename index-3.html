    Our History of 1985

    Online food ordering is the process of ordering food, for delivery or pickup, from a website or other application. The product can be either ready-to-eat food or food that has not been specially prepared for direct consumption . Online food delivery through third-party companies have emerged as a global industry, leading to a "delivery revolution.From 1985 to 2015, global revenues for the online food delivery sector rose from $90 billion to $294 billion.
